Types of Home Insurance Coverage

At SD Group of Companies, we offer a range of home insurance coverage options to ensure comprehensive protection:

  • Building Insurance: Covers physical damage to your home structure.
  • Contents Insurance: Protects against loss or damage to your personal belongings.
  • Liability Insurance: Covers legal liabilities for injury or damage to third parties on your property.
  • Landlord Insurance: Covers rental properties and protects against tenant-related risks.
